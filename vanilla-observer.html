02. Passive GPU Parallax (Compositor Only)

PASSIVE SCROLL BINDING

No Frame Rendering Blockages

Scroll up and down. Notice how the image on the left slides within its container. Rather than listening directly to window scroll to apply raw coordinates (which stops main-thread layouts), we use a **passive scroll listener** that injects a CSS variable `--scroll-offset`.

The browser's layout compositor processes this translation independently of standard JS execution times. This is the optimal practice for high-traffic, luxury landing pages.
